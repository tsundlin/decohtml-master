ABOUT US

Our project

Team Full Mark Plus One

Since the invention of motor vehicle, driving has become an essential part in people's life. Most of us chose to commute via driving, simply because it is convenient, fast, efficient, and easy. You are able to save a tremendous amount of time if you choose to drive. However, many car accidents occurred every year due to inexperienced driver, young driver, elderly driver, and driver who is afraid of surrounding traffic. With this in mind, our team, Full Marks Plus One, has decided to create and design a website that can help educate drivers to be more vigilant. As most accidents occurred due to driver failing to identify hazards, by educating drivers the ability to identify hazards, it may prove useful to reduce the number of car accidents occurred every year. Our main goal is to through educating drivers, have them exposed to a large variety of high hazard scenarios, in order to improve drivers ability to perceive hazards in the real life. By also implementing driving simulation game into the website, allowig a more immersive exerpience for the user to experience, is what makes our website unique and set apart from the other competitions.
